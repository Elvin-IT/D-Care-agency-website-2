About D-care Agency

At D-care Agency, we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services that enhance the quality of life for our clients. Our mission is to deliver compassionate, professional care in the comfort of your own home.

With years of experience in the healthcare industry, our team of certified professionals is committed to maintaining the highest standards of care. We believe in treating each client as part of our extended family, ensuring personalized attention and support.

Our core values include integrity, compassion, professionalism, and community service. We strive to make a positive difference in the lives of those we serve while building lasting relationships based on trust and respect.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services designed to meet your individual needs and promote your well-being.

Home Healthcare

Professional care in the comfort of your home. Our caregivers provide assistance with daily activities, medication reminders, and companionship.

Physical Therapy

Specialized rehabilitation services to help you regain strength, mobility, and independence after injury or surgery.

Nursing Services

Comprehensive nursing care provided by licensed professionals for complex medical conditions and post-hospital care.

Medical Consultation

Expert medical advice and guidance from our team of specialists to help you make informed healthcare decisions.
